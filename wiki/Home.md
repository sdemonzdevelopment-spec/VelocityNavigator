# Welcome to VelocityNavigator V3.0

> [!NOTE]  
> VelocityNavigator is built exclusively for Velocity `3.x` and requires Java `17+`. It is engineered for maximum performance.

VelocityNavigator isn't just a `/lobby` command replacer. It's a fully intelligent, context-aware traffic multiplexer for your Minecraft network.

By moving your backend traffic logic directly into the proxy, players are dynamically sorted, balanced, and verified before they ever hit the heavy game servers, saving you massive amounts of infrastructure overhead.
